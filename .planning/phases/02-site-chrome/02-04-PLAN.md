    STEP 1 — AnnouncementBar (CHR-01):
    a. Add an AnnouncementBar section to a page. Confirm it renders a navy bar with default locationLabel "SD | República de Colombia", changeLabel "Cambiar" (with ChevronDown), and followLabel "Síguenos en:".
    b. Edit each text field in the sidebar — confirm the bar updates live.
    c. Add 2-3 social-link blocks. Confirm they appear to the right of the follow label in a horizontal row.
    d. Resize the browser to mobile width (less than 1024px). Confirm the bar stacks or adjusts gracefully — no horizontal overflow.

    STEP 2 — SiteHeader (CHR-02):
    a. Add a SiteHeader section. Confirm it renders a navy header with a logo placeholder (ImageGuard gray box) and an account button ("Mi Cuenta") on the right.
    b. Add 4-5 nav-link blocks. Set labels and enable hasCaret on two of them. Confirm the nav links appear with ChevronDown icons (visual only — clicking a caret does not open a dropdown).
    c. At desktop (1024px and above): confirm nav links display inline horizontally. The mobile hamburger is not visible.
    d. At mobile (less than 1024px): confirm only the hamburger icon is visible. Click the hamburger — the nav drawer opens showing the nav links. Click again — drawer closes. Press Enter or Space on the summary element — confirm keyboard toggle works.
    e. Upload a logo image via the sidebar. Confirm ImageGuard placeholder is replaced by the logo.
    f. Edit accountLabel and accountUrl — confirm the account button updates.

    STEP 3 — Footer (CHR-03):
    a. Add a Footer section. Confirm it renders a navy footer with a logo placeholder, a mixed block area (EmptyState shown when no blocks), and a legal row with default copyright, "Términos y condiciones", and "Políticas de privacidad" links.
    b. Add 2 footer-column blocks. Set titles and 2-3 links each. Confirm columns appear side by side in the block band. Confirm empty link slots (no label) render no link element.
    c. Add 1 store-badge block (App Store or Google Play). Confirm it appears alongside the footer-column blocks in the same band.
    d. Add 1 social-link block. Confirm it appears in the same block band.
    e. Resize to mobile — confirm footer stacks single-column gracefully.
    f. Edit copyright and terms/privacy labels + URLs in the sidebar. Confirm legal row updates live.

    STEP 4 — Registry / Contract sanity:
    Run: yarn test:contract — confirm exits 0 (green)
